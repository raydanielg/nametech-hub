ENTERPRISE FAQ
Question	Answer
Can we get a custom quote that combines multiple services?	Yes – we create tailored enterprise packages. Contact our Enterprise team.
Do you offer government/NGO discounts?	Yes – verified NGOs and government agencies get 15-25% discount.
What is the minimum contract duration?	For most enterprise services, minimum is 6 months. For training, no minimum.
Do we own the intellectual property?	For custom software and IaaS, yes – you own everything we build for you.
Can we visit NAMTECH-HUB before signing?	Absolutely – we offer free site tours for enterprise prospects.
How do we pay?	Bank transfer, credit card, or government purchase order.
Is there a free trial for enterprise services?	For Training: we offer one free workshop (2 hours). For other services: paid pilot available.
Can we start small and scale up?	Yes – many enterprise clients start with a pilot project worth $10k-20k.
